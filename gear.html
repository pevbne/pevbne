Gear

What kind of gear do I need for riding?

Helmets

Required For: check All

Entry Level:

These are fine for casual cruising, but won't protect your face if you come off. Better than nothing in most cases.
Type: Skate Helmet
Cost: Around $60-90

Middle Tier:

These are what Mountain Bikers use, the entry level versions of these are pretty good at protecting your face.
Type: Entry Level MX Helmet
Cost: Around $120-150

Top Tier:

These are high end Mountain Biking and Downhill helmets, those that support MIPS will help keep your noggin from getting scrambled in a crash.
Type: MX Helmet + MIPS/Guard
Cost: Around $200-400

Gloves

Recommended For: check Skateboard check Scooter

Entry Level

These just help prevent callouses on the hands for scooters and lessen remote and handlebar deathgrip strain.
Type: Cycling Gloves
Cost: Around $12-50

Middle Tier

Padded gloves with slide pads on the palm to allow you to slide to a stop without tearing up your hands.
Type: Clothing with Padding/D3O Inserts
Cost: Around $200-250AUD

Top Tier

These are very popular in the esk8 groups. They have replaceable pads for sliding.
Type: Skate Slide Gloves
Cost: Around $150AUD

Wrist Protection

Required For: check Skate, EUC and

Entry Level:

These are fine for casual cruising, but aren't too comfortable.
Type: Skate Wrist Guards
Cost: Around $25

Middle Tier:

These are 'pro' grade gear, will survive many crashes..
Type: Pro Skate Wrist Guards
Cost: Around $50-90

Top Tier:

These are higher end and higher quality, targeted at Snowboarders and EUC Riders.
Type: Snowboarding/EUC Wrist Support
Cost: Around $150

Knee Protection

Recommended For: check All

Entry Level:

These are fine for casual cruising, but aren't too comfortable. Tend to be sold in packs of Knee, Wrist and Elbow guards.
Type: 'Safety Pads Kits'
Cost: Around $25-60

Middle Tier:

Higher quality, higher cost with a little more comfortability.
Cost: Around $100-120

Top Tier:

This is 'pro' grade Motocross gear
Cost: Around $150

Recommended For: check Skateboard check Scooter

Armoured Clothing
