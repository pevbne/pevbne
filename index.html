Welcome to PEVBNE!

What is this site?

PEVBNE has been built as a resource for all Personal Electric Vehicle riders and users in Brisbane.

What's a PEV?

PEV stands for Personal Electric Vehicle. They can also be called:

  • Personal Mobility Devices
  • Rideables
  • Last Mile Mobility
There are several main classes of PEV available these days, these include:

Electric Scooters(EScoot)

Electric Skateboards (ESK8/Eskate)

Electric Unicycles (EUC)
